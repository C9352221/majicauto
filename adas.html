Advanced Driver Assistance Systems

What Is ADAS?

ADAS — Advanced Driver Assistance Systems — is the network of cameras, radar units, and sensors that power modern safety features like automatic emergency braking, lane-keep assist, adaptive cruise control, and blind-spot monitoring.

After a windshield replacement or collision repair, these cameras and sensors must be precisely recalibrated to manufacturer specifications. Even a small deviation can cause safety systems to react incorrectly — or fail to react at all.

Majic Auto performs both static and dynamic ADAS calibrations for all makes and models, ensuring your safety systems work exactly as intended before you leave our shop.
