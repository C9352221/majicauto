Why Ceramic Coating

Built-In Protection

Every Ceramic Pro application delivers four core benefits that keep your vehicle looking and performing its best.

Hydrophobic

Water beads on contact and rolls off the surface, carrying dirt and contaminants with it. Washing your vehicle has never been easier.

UV Protection

The coating blocks harmful UV rays that break down clear coat over time, preventing oxidation, fading, and chalking on your vehicle's paint.

Chemical Resistance

Bird droppings, tree sap, bug splatter, and road chemicals are repelled rather than etching into your paint — protecting your finish from daily hazards.

Enhanced Gloss

Ceramic Pro amplifies your vehicle's existing paint depth, delivering a rich, mirror-like finish that turns heads and maintains showroom quality for years.
