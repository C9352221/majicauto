More Than Repairs

Complete Automotive Solutions

Majic Auto offers more than collision and mechanical repairs for your fleet. We also provide windshield and auto glass replacement for fleet vehicles — getting your drivers back behind the wheel fast after a chip, crack, or full break.

Modern fleet vehicles equipped with Advanced Driver Assistance Systems require ADAS recalibration after any windshield replacement. Our technicians handle both glass replacement and ADAS calibration in a single appointment, eliminating the need for a second shop visit.

One relationship, one invoice, complete service — for every vehicle in your fleet.
